# Tsuki – Product Overview

Tsuki is an Android menstrual cycle tracking app. The name "Tsuki" means "moon" in Japanese, reflecting the lunar connection to menstrual cycles.

## Current State
The app is in early development, covering the onboarding and sign-up flows only. No cycle tracking logic has been implemented yet.

## User Flow (implemented)
1. **Splash** – branded loading screen (2s delay)
2. **Onboarding** – 3-screen carousel introducing the app
3. **Choice** – entry point offering Sign Up or Sign In
4. **Sign Up Choice** – email/password registration form with social auth options (Google, Apple, Facebook)
5. **Name** – collect user's name (post sign-up step)
6. **Birthday** – collect user's date of birth (post sign-up step)

## Target Audience
People who menstruate, seeking a clean and friendly cycle tracking experience.
